What's Food Finder?

Food Finder is exactly what it sounds like. It's a platform that connects people willing to give and those who willing to accept. It is meant to simplify the process of finding a place to donate, albeit be food, clothes, or toys. Through Food Finder, you can see which locations in your community offer certain services.
